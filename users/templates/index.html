Our Hotel

Set on a 2-hectare property with landscaped gardens, this upscale hotel is 1 km from the landmark Kathmandu Durbar Square and 5 km from Tribhuvan International Airport.

Short Details

The traditional, warmly decorated rooms feature minifridges and flat-screen TVs, plus tea and coffeemaking facilities. Upgraded rooms come with free Wi-Fi and/or sitting areas, while club rooms have access to a private lounge with drinks and snacks. Suites offer separate living and dining areas (1 has a kitchen).
